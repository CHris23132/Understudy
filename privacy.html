Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

At Understudy, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data and outlines our practices regarding the collection, use, and safeguarding of your information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

Understudy does not collect or share any personal data with third parties. All information you provide within the app, including notes, profiles, and preferences, remains strictly on your device.

2. How Your Data Is Used

  • Data you enter is used momentarily to generate personalized models and insights via our AI API.
  • Once the AI model is generated, your data is not stored or retained by our servers or any third party.
  • All profiles, notes, and generated insights are stored locally on your device and are never uploaded or shared externally.

3. Data Security

We employ industry-standard security measures to protect your information. Because all data is stored locally, you have full control over your information. We recommend keeping your device secure and up to date to further protect your data.

4. No Third-Party Sharing

Understudy does not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to outside parties. We do not use third-party analytics or advertising services that track your activity.

5. Children's Privacy

Understudy is not intended for use by children under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect or solicit personal information from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
